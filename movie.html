Synopsis

Johnnie Gray has two loves in his life — his locomotive "The General" and his sweetheart Annabelle Lee. When the Civil War breaks out, Annabelle's family pressures Johnnie to enlist in the Confederate Army, but he is rejected because he is more valuable as an engineer. Feeling humiliated, Annabelle accuses him of cowardice. When Union spies steal his beloved locomotive with Annabelle on board, Johnnie sets off on a daring pursuit to rescue both. Buster Keaton's masterpiece of physical comedy combines breathtaking stunt work with genuine emotional stakes — a film that has never been surpassed for sheer cinematic invention.
